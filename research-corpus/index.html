Scope and interpretation

A bridge across research layers—not a claim of physical validation.

Status discipline: a SymPy PASS means an encoded algebraic, dimensional, logical, numerical, limiting, or consistency check passed under declared assumptions. It is not automatically a Lean proof or empirical validation. PROVED is reserved for named declarations accepted by the Lean kernel.

The JSON manifest currently exposes four representative cross-layer bridge objects while linking to the complete 142-object registries. Stable IDs must be preserved exactly; master objects, canonical equations, and supporting objects are related but must not be silently collapsed.

E17 · alias M2

Nonsingular curvature-feedback operator

SymPy: PASS Lean: OPEN

Rε(ψ) = ψ̄ / (|ψ|² + ε²e−2α|ψ|²)
Θε[ψ] = −(Δψ)Rε(ψ)

The corrected modulus-squared denominator is positive for ε > 0. Lean presently formalizes a counterexample to the historical scalar denominator and restricted-sector repairs; the complete complex operator remains an open formalization target.

E12 · alias M3

Finite-band spectral relation

SymPy: PASS Lean: PROVED support

λgrow(k) = r + a|k|² − b|k|⁴
k★ = √(a / 2b)

The canonical notation uses λgrow rather than σ(k) to avoid collision with curvature σ. Lean proves the one-mode polynomial symbol through bandpass_oneMode_symbol, not the full nonlinear pattern-selection theorem.

E6 · aliases M1, E7

Density-weighted curvature mass relation

SymPy: PASS dimensions Lean: PROVED dimensions Physical identification: CONDITIONAL

Erest = ℏckeff
m = (ℏ/c)keff
m = (ℏ/c)⟨σ⟩w when E5 assumptions hold

SymPy and Lean support dimensional closure. They do not establish that physical mass is generated by curvature. The locked-curvature form also depends on the exact-locking and weighting assumptions in E5.

E70 · aliases M4, E24, E67

Spatial-dimensionality stability criterion

Embedding threshold: PASS E70 stability implication: CONDITIONAL Lean PDE coverage: OPEN

H²(Ω) ↪ L∞(Ω) when 2 > n/2

The Sobolev embedding threshold is supported. It is not represented as proof that every WCT equilibrium is stable for n ≤ 3 or unstable for n ≥ 4. Continuum Sobolev and PDE stability results remain outside exact Lean coverage.

Machine-reading rules

Required distinctions for reliable answers.

Preserve identity

  • Use canonical_id as the primary key.
  • Keep aliases and supporting objects distinct.
  • Do not infer equivalence from similar names alone.

Respect status precedence

  1. Per-object derived override
  2. Executable SymPy registry
  3. Canonical equation document
  4. Narrative paper text

Separate validation layers

  • Symbolic check ≠ Lean proof
  • Lean proof ≠ empirical validation
  • Publication ≠ independent replication

Do not invent missing data

Use null, empty arrays, OPEN, or an explicit unresolved status when a mapping, date, proof, or evidence record cannot be verified.
